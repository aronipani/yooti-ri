## Root Cause

Two pages fail to wire the existing `addItem` function from `CartContext` to their UI.

1. **`CataloguePage.tsx`** тАФ `<ProductGrid>` is rendered on both the search-results path
   and the regular catalogue path, but `onAddToCart` is never passed to either instance.
   `ProductCard`'s button calls `onAddToCart?.(product.id)` тАФ the `?` makes it silently a
   no-op when the prop is absent. Cart state is never updated.

2. **`ProductDetailPage.tsx`** тАФ The "Add to Cart" `<button>` has no `onClick` handler
   and does not call `useCart()` at all. Clicking does nothing.

`CartContext.addItem` is already correct тАФ it calls `cartApi.addToCart`, sets full cart
state, and opens the drawer. The fix is purely wiring at the page level.
