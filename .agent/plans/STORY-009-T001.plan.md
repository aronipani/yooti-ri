## Summary

Build admin product management API: GET/POST/PUT/DELETE /api/v1/admin/products and POST /admin/products/:id/images. All endpoints require admin role (verified server-side from JWT). Block deletion of products with pending orders. Image upload validated for type (image/*) and size (<=5MB) before storage.
